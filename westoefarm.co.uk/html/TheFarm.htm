The Farm

Westoe Farm is a 1650 acre family owned and run arable farm growing milling wheat for Warburton's bread and sugar beet for British sugar as a few of our crops. We have a family shoot on the farm. We also have a 99.47 KWatt solar farm on our grain store which reduces our carbon footprint by 46 tonnes per year.

    Tennis Court

    T

    There is a Tennis Court that guests may use. This is also a family used court so please accept it is out of use if the family or friends are using it. Please ask before using the court. Proper tennis shoes must be worn.

    If you need to borrow rackets and balls then please ask us. However, they must be returned to us and not left on the court. All tennis balls that land in the swimming pool must be retrieved.

    All children must be accompanied by an adult at all times on the tennis court as it has a close proximity to the swimming pool.

    Westoe Farm will not accept any liability for any injury sustained by guests whilst using the Tennis Court and other facilties.

    Swimming Pool

    There is an outside pool, which is heated in the summer for guests to use during the summer months. This is a family pool so it may be in use by the family and their friends - it is not exclusively for guests.

    Please ask before you use the pool, so we can open it and clean it before you use it.. Please ask for swimming towels and do not take your bath towels out to the pool.

    All children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult at all times around the swimming pool as it is unfenced and un-guarded; it is part of the family garden.

    Westoe Farm will not accept any liability for any loss or injury sustained by guests whilst using the swimming pool and other facilities.
